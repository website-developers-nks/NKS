NK Securities Research is a high-frequency proprietary trading firm built at the intersection of technology, data science and global financial markets. Founded in 2011 and headquartered in Gurugram-India, the firm has steadily evolved into a sophisticated trading organisation with a growing international presence. Powered by the in-house ultra low latency infrastructure platform and ultra-low latency profit-making strategies, we have one of the fastest trading systems. We operate in the most competitive arenas globally - where success is defined by speed, precision, and innovation at scale.

WHAT WE DO

The tech behind the trade

High-Frequency Trading

Ultra-low latency, in-house built tech stack capturing market inefficiencies with thousands of orders per second. Proprietary algorithms tested and tuned over 15+ years of market experience.

Quantitative Research

Petabyte-scale data infrastructure and Millions compute cores power large-scale research and backtesting across Indian and global markets.

Risk & Infrastructure

In-house real-time risk engine with hard stop-loss controls and system monitoring. All systems are built with risk mitigation that limits maximum daily exposure.
