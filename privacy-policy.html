Privacy Policy

NK Securities Research Private Limited and its affiliates are committed to protecting personal information when you use our website. This policy governs the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data submitted to NKS Research Singapore, including through www.nksecurities.com, and explains our compliance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

Collection of Personal Data

Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, contact number, email address, physical address, gender
  • Career demographics: job title, employer, skills, experience, qualifications
  • Documents provided: resumes, CVs, remuneration details, photos, videos
  • Information related to business purposes

How We Use Your Data

  • Responding to individual requests or stated collection purposes
  • Maintaining contact with vendors and business contacts
  • Keeping vendors informed of company developments and updates
  • General management and reporting (invoicing, account management)
  • Recruitment purposes
  • Employment-related services and internal functions
  • Other business-related purposes

Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose personal data to third parties except when required by law, with individual consent, or when engaging third parties as data intermediaries or subcontractors. Such third parties are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality.

Data may be disclosed to affiliated firms where necessary to meet purposes for which information was submitted or to provide relevant future communications.

Data Subject Rights

Access and Correction

Upon request, we provide access to personal data or correct errors/omissions in accordance with PDPA requirements.

Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw consent with reasonable notice. We will cease collection, use, or disclosure unless required by law.

Data Protection Standards

Accuracy

We make reasonable efforts to ensure personal data accuracy and completeness.

Security

Generally accepted technology and operational security standards protect data against unauthorized access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, or disposal. Personnel follow network-wide security policies; only authorized staff access personally identifiable information under confidentiality agreements.

Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for collection purposes and legal/business requirements.

International Transfers

Any transfers outside Singapore comply with PDPA standards ensuring comparable protection.

Website Privacy

This policy applies to personal data collected via our websites. Cookies may be used to provide customized experiences. Visitors may decline cookies in browser settings without significantly impairing navigation, though some functionality may be limited.
